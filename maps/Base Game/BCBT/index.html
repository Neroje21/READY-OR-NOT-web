Buy Cheap, Buy Twice / Caesar's Cars Dealership

Buy Cheap, Buy Twice is the fifteenth playable mission in Ready or Not. It takes place on February 10, 2026 at a rather large car dealership beside an extremely run down street, in which both Los Locos and the Russian Mafia have made a base for themselves. The map consists of three main areas ; The interior of the two-floor dealership, the car lot outside, and the mechanic shop behind the dealership. Enemies can be found in any of these. A few civilians are also present.
