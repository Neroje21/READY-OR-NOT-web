GA51

The GA51 is a compact, hard-hitting battle rifle, offering the firepower of a 7.62x51mm round in a much smaller frame. Its shortened barrel increases recoil and muzzle flash, requiring skill and control to handle the intense kick and concussion, especially in close-quarters or indoor enviroments.
