M14S-16

The M14 is a Classic American battle rifle chambered in 7.62x51mm, combining range, firepower and precision. While it excels in semi-automatic mode, its fast cyclic rate makes it notoriously difficult to controll in full-auto, limiting its practicality for automatic fire in most combat situations. This variant sports a shorter barrel and is equipped with rail system for mounting various attachments.
