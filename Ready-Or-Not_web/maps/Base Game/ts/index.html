The Spider / Brixley talent time

The Spider is the fourth mission in Ready or Not. On June 4, 2025, a number of anonymous tips by local residents report suspicious activity at a construction yard owned by the Brixley Talent production company. Repeated attempts to contact and serve a search warrant to the company have been rebuffed by their legal team. The Chief of Police has dispatched D Platoon to serve a no-knock search warrant of the residence, and to detain anyone present.
