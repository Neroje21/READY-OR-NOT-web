Elephant / Watt Community college

Elephant is the eleventh (ninth chronologically) playable mission in Ready or Not. On October 17, 2025, four students commit a mass-shooting at Watt Community College, presumably due to a mixture of mental health issues and immense dissatisfaction with the current state of the United States. LSPD is able to contain the shooters in the Science Wing and D Platoon is sent in to neutralize the threat.
