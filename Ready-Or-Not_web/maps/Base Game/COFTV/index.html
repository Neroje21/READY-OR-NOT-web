Carriers of the Vine / Cherryessa Farm

Carriers of the Vine is the sixteenth mission in Ready or Not. It has D Platoon being dispatched on June 1, 2025, to pacify a new age cult committing acts of vigilantism across Los Suenos. The mission takes place on a large rural property in the countryside of Los Suenos, featuring a main house, several outbuildings, and a large vineyard. The cultists are hostile and will defend their property aggressively.
