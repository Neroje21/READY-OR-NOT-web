Ides of march / Brisa Cove

Ides of March is the sixth playable mission in Ready or Not. A group of domestic terrorists have occupied the luxury Brisa Cove Apartments, taking several residents hostage. On October 1, 2025, D-Platoon have been dispatched to neutralize the threat and defuse the situation ahead of the Senator's upcoming presidential campaign.
