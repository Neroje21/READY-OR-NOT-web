TRPL

The TRPL is a compact, pistol-sized pepperball launcher, firing high-impact CS gas rounds to disable threats without lethal force. Modeled after traditional duty pistols, it provides law enforcement and security personnel with a familiar, effective toll for crowd control and suspect compiliance. While effective at neutralizing targets, its limited 6-round capacity means careful aim placement is advised.
